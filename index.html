History

A recipe decades in the making.

When Candace married into the Tucker family, she discovered their delicious birthday tradition: angel food cake. This began with Grandma Barbara, whose love for angel food cake made it the centerpiece of countless family celebrations.

For years, Candace her new family baked angel food cakes from box mixes. After moving to Idaho, where the mixes were hard to come by, she decided to learn how to make them completely from scratch.

What started as a necessity became a passion. Today every Candy's Classic Cake is made from scratch using the same love and tradition that inspired those family celebrations.
